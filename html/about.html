Our Story

Cashwell Irrigation Repair & Lighting Design is a small, owner-operated business built around the same values that guide Aaron in his firefighting career: integrity, reliability, and calm problem-solving. Every system he touches gets the kind of care he’d expect for his own home.

About twenty years ago, Aaron started helping friends and neighbors with landscaping and quickly fell in love with the work. What began as a way to lend a hand slowly grew into a dedicated service focused on irrigation repair and landscape lighting design for homeowners who want their yards to both work and look their best.

Customers often mention the same things after working with Aaron: he shows up when he says he will, explains what he finds in plain language, and recommends only what’s actually needed. There’s no hard sell—just clear options, honest pricing, and clean workmanship.

Services at a glance

We specialize in irrigation repair and low-voltage landscape lighting—with straightforward recommendations, clean work, and no surprises.

  • Irrigation troubleshooting and repairs (leaks, broken heads, valves, wiring, controllers)

  • Seasonal maintenance plans available (spring start-up, mid-season checkups, winterization)
